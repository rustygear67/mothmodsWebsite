Policies

Refund & Dispute Policy: All services are quoted and agreed upon before work begins. If you are unsatisfied with completed work, please contact us within 7 days of project completion and we will work with you to resolve the issue. Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Cancellation Policy: Appointments and scheduled work may be cancelled or rescheduled with at least 24 hours notice at no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee equal to the cost of the scheduled time. Rescheduling withing the 24 hour window will be done on a case-by-case basis
